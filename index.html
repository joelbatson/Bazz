How to Buy Bazz

1

Create a Wallet

Download MetaMask or Trust Wallet from your app store or browser extension store.

2

Fund Your Wallet

Buy Ethereum (ETH) or Binance Coin (BNB) from an exchange like Coinbase or Binance and send it to your wallet.

3

Connect to DEX

Go to a decentralized exchange (DEX) like Uniswap (for ETH) or PancakeSwap (for BNB) in your wallet's browser.

4

Swap for BAZZ

Paste the Bazz Coin contract address into the DEX, set your slippage, and swap your ETH/BNB for BAZZ!
